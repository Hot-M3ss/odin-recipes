Fried Cooriz

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes from the region of Kamani in Ezia. It's simple, easy, and fairly tasty. If you're not a fan of Jaloi petals, they can be subsituted with rose, or lavender petals.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of crushed/powdered Jaloi petals
  • 1/2 cup of Gumori paste
  • 1 cup of Uloo milk
  • 1 cup of breading (preferrably fonae)
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Pour 1/2 of the Uloo milk into a mixing bowl with the Gumori paste and mix well. Once it begins to thicken add in the Jaloi petals and the rest of the Uloo milk and stir until it begins to pull apart into strings.
  2. Break the mix into ping pong sized balls and roll them inside of the fonae breading. Once breaded, lower them into the pot of vegetable oil (on medium-high heat) for 1-2 minutes or until golden brown.
  3. Finally, plate them and then recognize that you just got pranked; April Fools!
